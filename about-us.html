About Us.

Welcome to the H2O Revolution!

Once upon a time in 2004, in the bustling heart of Tambaram, Chennai, a startup was born to conquer water impurities and bring pure, delicious water to every household and business.

Fast forward 20 years, and we've become the seasoned heroes of water purification with the magic of reverse osmosis.

We tackle both household and commercial needs, even taming the wildest water with our trusty water softeners. We handle both sales and service—your one-stop-shop for pure water!

Our secret weapon? The mighty Hi-Tech brand. Hi-Tech started by importing purification plants from the USA but soon began assembling them in India to improve service and availability.

To boost efficiency and cut costs, Hi-Tech began producing essential parts needed for domestic water purifiers. As demand grew, Hi-Tech expanded production and set up units across the country.

Every purifier we craft comes with a Total Dissolved Solid adjuster, ensuring water is treated to perfection, retaining essential salts and minerals for super healthy water.

Hi-Tech doesn't just stop at households. We provide clean water to schools, colleges, hospitals, and remote villages.

Our ultimate quest? To provide you with water so pure, it has 0% unwanted minerals and 100% satisfaction. Join us in making every drop count!
